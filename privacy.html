Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Blackout Space Weather does not collect, store, or sell personal information.

Data Sources

The app connects to NASA and NOAA data services to retrieve current space weather conditions and recent solar events.

Technical Information

Requests to NASA and NOAA may include standard technical information such as your IP address, device type, and request time. This information is handled by those external services according to their own privacy policies.

Accounts, Tracking, and Advertising

Blackout Space Weather does not require an account, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not use personal data for advertising.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy notice, contact the app developer through the support information listed on the App Store.
