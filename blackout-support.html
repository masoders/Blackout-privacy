Space weather, translated.

Blackout displays public NASA and NOAA space weather information as simple operational risk levels for geomagnetic storms, radio blackouts, and solar radiation storms.

What the app shows

  • Geomagnetic storm activity
  • Radio blackout risk
  • Solar radiation storm levels
  • Recent solar flare and CME events
  • Plain-language impact summaries

Data sources

  • NASA DONKI API
  • NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center public feeds
